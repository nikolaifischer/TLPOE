About

The Largest Planet On Earth has been bringing varied alternative rock to Munich stages since 2016. With influences from punk, pop, desert rock and grunge, the trio presents an atmospheric mixture of driving guitar riffs, groovy beats and occasional sing-along passages.

Nikolai Fischer (guitar, vocals), Thiemo Kersting (bass, synthi) and Felix Plankermann (drums, vocals) have been music enthusiasts and good friends since school days. Their different musical tastes help them to find their way into collective songwriting.

TLPOE performed in the finals of the Muc-King and Running for the Best Contests in 2017. In April 2018 the band released their first three-track album MOON.
